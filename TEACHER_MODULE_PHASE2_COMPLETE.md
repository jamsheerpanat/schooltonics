- **Real-time Marking**: Optimized interface for quick attendance marking on any device.

### 3. Performance Analytics & Insights
- **Interactive Charts**: Powered by Recharts, visualize attendance trends and grade distributions.
- **Class Comparison**: Compare performance across different classes taught by the teacher.
- **AI-Driven Action Items**: Proactive notifications for attendance drops or high performers.

### 4. Advanced Gradebook
- **Matrix View**: A spreadsheet-like grid showing all students and their scores for all assignments.
- **Sticky Headers**: Scroll horizontally through many assignments while keeping student names visible.
- **Auto-Averages**: Real-time calculation of student overall percentages.

### 5. Communication Hub
- **Class Broadcasts**: Post announcements to specific sections or all classes at once.
- **Targeted Audience**: Select whether to notify students, parents, or both.
- **Priority Labeling**: Highlight urgent updates for maximum visibility.

### 6. Classroom Management
- **Subject-Specific Detail**: Deep dive into any class to see student rosters, demographics, and performance stats.
- **Enhanced Dashboard**: A mission-control view featuring today's schedule, quick stats, and recent activity.
